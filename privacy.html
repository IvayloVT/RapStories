Your Privacy Matters

Rap Stories is a content website and archive. This sample privacy page explains what basic information may be collected when visitors browse the site or contact the team.

What we collect

This site may collect basic details you choose to submit, such as your name, email address, and message through the contact form. Standard technical data such as browser type, device type, and page visits may also be collected by hosting providers or analytics tools.

How information is used

  • To respond to contact requests and story submissions.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To maintain site security and prevent abuse.

Third-party services

If you add analytics, forms, fonts, embeds, or newsletters later, those services may process visitor data under their own privacy terms. Update this page whenever new tools are added.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact page.
