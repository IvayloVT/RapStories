Your Privacy Matters

Rap Stories respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using the website.

Introduction

Welcome to Rap Stories. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website, browse our content, or contact us through forms and other features.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Messages, story submissions, or other information you send through the contact form

We may also automatically collect certain technical information, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the site
  • Referring website or source

How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, submissions, and messages
  • Improve website performance, design, and user experience
  • Monitor and protect the website against spam, abuse, and security threats
  • Understand how visitors use the site and improve content strategy

Advertising & Cookies

Rap Stories may use Google AdSense and other third-party advertising services to display ads. These services may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to provide advertisements based on your visit to this website and other websites.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. These cookies help provide more relevant advertising based on your interests and browsing behavior.

Google may use the DoubleClick cookie and related advertising technologies. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

By using this website, you understand and agree that cookies and similar technologies may be used for analytics, functionality, and advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as form providers, analytics tools, embedded media, fonts, hosting services, and advertising partners. These services may collect, process, and store data according to their own privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services that may interact with your data through this website.

Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights (UK & EU Users)

If you are located in the United Kingdom or the European Economic Area, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data where appropriate
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To make a privacy-related request, please contact us through the contact page.

Children’s Privacy

Rap Stories is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the website, please contact us so we can review and remove it if necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our site, services, legal requirements, or advertising partners. Any updates will be posted on this page with the latest version available here.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to make a privacy-related request, please use the contact page.
