How the Site Should Feel

This style works well for a hip hop archive because it feels premium, serious, and visually memorable.

Design direction

Use a dark background, gold accents, bold typography, large artist images, and spacious cards. This gives the site a modern “museum of hip hop” look.

Add artist thumbnails, category filters, and dedicated profile pages like 2pac.html, biggie.html, or nas.html.

Best page structure

A strong homepage usually follows this order:

Hero → Search → Featured artists → Categories → Newest stories → Timeline/history → Newsletter → Footer
